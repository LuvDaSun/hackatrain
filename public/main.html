What is Hackatrain?

Hackatrain is the first hackathon on a train in the Netherlands. This unforgettable experience will bring 50 teams of 3 hackers together to make history and share an exciting ride, while building innovative solutions to make traveling by train more pleasant. The NS (provides) data and APIs which you can use. Departing Amsterdam, Hackatrain takes off on July 14, 2015 to Berlin arriving at Tech Open Air (TOA).

What is TOA?

Tech Open Air (TOA) is Europes leading interdisciplinary tech festival, which brings together tech, music, art and science in Berlin, July 15-16-17, 2015. By bringing together technological changemakers with other disciplines, TOA helps the disrupted better anticipate the future and the technologist better understand the world they change.
