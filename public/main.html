What is Hackatrain?

Hackatrain is the first hackathon on a train in the Netherlands. This unforgettable experience will bring 50 teams of 3 hackers together to make history and share an exciting ride, while building innovative solutions to make traveling by train more pleasant. The NS (provides) data and APIs which you can use. Departing Amsterdam, Hackatrain takes off on July 14, 2015 to Berlin arriving at Tech Open Air (TOA).

What is TOA?

Tech Open Air (TOA) is Europes leading interdisciplinary tech festival, which brings together tech, music, art and science in Berlin, July 15-16-17, 2015. By bringing together technological changemakers with other disciplines, TOA helps the disrupted better anticipate the future and the technologist better understand the world they change.

Ordina is the largest independent services provider in the field of consulting, solutions and IT in the Benelux. We focus on the financial services sector, public sector, healthcare sector and a number of specific segments in the industry sector.

NS (Dutch Railways) is the principal passenger railway operator in the Netherlands providing rail services on the Dutch main rail network. NS runs 4,800 scheduled domestic trains a day, serving 1.1 million passengers. In addition, NS provides international rail services from the Netherlands to other European destinations and carries out concessions on a number of foreign rail markets.

The Amsterdam Creative Industries Network (ACIN) is the Dutch national talent, knowledge and business network for creative industry and digital technology. ACIN was founded by the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences, the Amsterdam School of the Arts and Inholland University of Applied Sciences.

What is Hackatrain?

Hackatrain is the first hackathon on a train in the Netherlands. This unforgettable experience will bring 50 teams of 3 hackers together to make history and share an exciting ride, while building innovative solutions to make traveling by train more pleasant. The NS (Dutch Railways) provides data and APIs which you can use. Hackatrain is recruiting awesome developers, designers, and entrepreneurs for this revolutionary high speed hack. Jump aboard to travel the world and join the inaugural Hackatrain. Departing Amsterdam, the Hackatrain takes off on 14.7.2015 to Berlin arriving at Tech Open Air - Europes leading interdisciplinary tech festival which brings together tech, music, art and science.

Who is organizing Hackatrain?

My name is Amal Whyte. I am an event organiser and marketer based in Amsterdam. The events I organize are published under By Whyte and is the official event organization for the 2015 Hackatrain.

I came up with the idea to organize a hackathon on a train last year, when I was taking a train from Brussels to London with some friends. I thought it would be super fun to jump aboard with some likeminded people to work on a challenging case, collaborate and exchange perspectives, and develop some really cool ideas. This year I am putting the Hackatrain concept into action, bringing a new concept and creative people to one of the coolest tech festivals in Europe: Tech Open Air. What case will I be working on? We kick off with a very cool innovative challenge brought by the NS (the Dutch Railways). You find more details under case {{linken naar pagina met case uitleg}} in the menu on the homepage.

Can I win something?

Yes you can! The NS (the Dutch Railways) will provide a train ticket around Europe for each member of the winning team.

How are the teams chosen and developed?

We are dividing all Hackatrain enrollments up in groups of 3 hackers. If you have your own kick ass team, then please send an email to: info@hackatrain.nl, providing names and professions.

Can I sign up with my own team of 3?

If you have your own kick ass team of 3 hackers, please send an email to: info@hackatrain.nl, providing all names and professions.

When is Hackatrain?

Hackatrain will depart on Tuesday July 14, 2015 and arrive on Wednesday July 15, 2015. Your ticket includes a three day entrance to Tech Open Air Berlin {{link to www.toaberlin.com}}

Logistics

When do I have to commit by?

As soon as possible!.with registration closing June 16th. On the 17th of June there will be a pre Hackatrain get together In Amsterdam, organized by our partner Ordina. There will be limited tickets available.

How do I get my train ticket?

We are starting the Hackatrain with a pre-programme at the Amsterdam Knowledge Mile (Amsterdam Amstel Station). This is where official registration will take place after your online ticket purchase and where you get your official train ticket to enter the Hackatrain.

From where is the train departing?

Hackatrain departs from Amsterdam Central Station. However, our programme starts at a secret location - the Amsterdam Knowledge Mile (Amsterdam Amstel Station).

How many tickets are available for the Hackatrain?

In this years edition we will sell a maximum of 150 tickets for the Hackatrain.

All ticket sales are non-refundable.

The train

What about Wifi?

NS (Dutch Railways), who introduced the Hackatrain case will also be providing USB data sticks with data available offline, just in case the Internet is not working properly during the journey.

What about charging my notebook?

Unfortunately there are no power point on the Hackatrain. Therefore make sure your notebook is fully charged.

What about the return trip?

Your return is July 18th, after the close of Tech Open Air Berlin. No event will be hosted on the return.
